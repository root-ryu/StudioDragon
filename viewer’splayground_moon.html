1st Video Contest Winners

Fans reimagined the iconic scenes of “The Glory”.
We celebrate the creativity and passion of our contest winners.

Gold Award

  • Title : Typo of Sorry

  • Nickname : rainyroom

  • Acceptance comment:
    “I wanted to express how a single word, or even a typo, can hold deep emotions—regret, forgiveness, and pain. The drama The Glory inspired me to explore the power of unspoken apologies. Winning the Gold Award feels surreal, and I’m grateful to everyone who connected with my work. This moment reminds me that even small mistakes can lead to meaningful stories.”

Silver Award

  • Title : The Fire Inside

  • Nickname : bloom

  • Acceptance comment:
    “I truly enjoyed watching The Glory, and many of its scenes stayed with me long after. I created this video inspired by the unspoken emotions behind every message sent—or left unsent. It’s an incredible honor to receive this award, and I’m thankful for the chance to share my interpretation. This experience motivates me to keep creating stories that connect with others.”

Popular Choice

  • Title : Read Receipt, Unsent

  • Nickname : mintlatte

  • Acceptance comment:

This project took a lot of effort and late nights, so being recognized by viewers means a lot to me. It’s heartwarming to know that people connected with my work. I’ll remember this encouragement and keep challenging myself to do even better.”

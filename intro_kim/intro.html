Studio Dragon, a subsidiary of global IP powerhouse CJ ENM, is a leading K-drama studio in South Korea and a group of premium storytellers who captivate the world with their limitless narratives.
  • Studio Dragon owns 266 global premium IPs, totaling 4,127 episodes. With eight drama production subsidiaries and affiliates and a team of 263 creators, including Korea's A-list writers, directors, and producers, Studio Dragon currently plans over 140 dramas and produces approximately 25 works annually, distributing them globally.
  • Led by "Sweet Home 1, 2, 3", the first Asian drama to rank in the Netflix Global TOP 10, numerous other works have also ranked, including 'The Glory 1, 2", "Queen of Tears", "Iduna!", "Gyeongseong Creature", "The Sound of the Sword", "Speed", "The Uncanny Counter 1, 2", "Ilta Scandal", "Shroop", "Little Women", "The Return of Superman 1, 2", "Crash Landing on You", and "It's Okay to Not Be Okay". They have also been invited to and nominated for various international events,including the Cannes Film Festival and the International Emmy Awards,and are spreading the joy of K-Drama around the world.
  • Furthermore, through the establishment of the Los Angeles International Office (2019) and Studio Dragon Japan (2022), the company is expanding its business globally. Starting with its first order for the Apple TV+ series "The Big Door Prize" with Skydance Media, the company is also working on various global projects with leading partners worldwide, including "Designers" (produced with Creator/EP "Soo Hu," UIS, UCP, and Ink Factory), and co-producing and planning numerous remakes of Studio Dragon IP, including "Crash Landing on You" and "Vincenzo." The company is making strides toward becoming a major global studio.
